AOTIC CRM -- Final Project Plan (Client Copy)

What We Are Building

A custom CRM platform built specifically for AOTIC's automotive customization business. This is NOT a generic CRM -- every feature is designed around your 6 verticals, your workflow, and your team structure.

Your 6 Business Verticals (All Supported)

Audio & Acoustics

Interior Themes & Custom Seat Designs

Sun Protection Film, PPF & Detailing

Base-to-Top OEM Upgrades

Custom Cores (Headlights, Conversions, Body Kits)

Lighting & Visibility Solutions

Module-by-Module: What You Will Get

MODULE 1: Login & User Management

Secure login for all staff (email + password)

Each person gets a role with specific permissions:

Owner / Super Admin sees everything, approves discounts, overrides locks

Branch Manager / Supervisor -- manages daily operations, assigns work, performs QC

Sales Executive -- handles leads, quotations, bookings

Workshop Technician sees only their assigned jobs, uploads photos, logs materials

QC Inspector -- fills QC checklists, signs off on completed work

Accounts / Finance -- invoicing, payments, Tally exports

Front Desk / Customer Support -- lead entry, communication logs

Each role sees ONLY what they need -- nothing more

All sensitive actions (discount overrides, invoice edits, payment changes) are logged with who did it and when

MODULE 2: Lead Management & Sales Pipeline

Lead Capture: Quick form -- customer name, phone, car model, service interest, budget, source (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, Google Ads, referral, walk-in)

Lead Temperature: Hot / Warm / Cold / Lost -- color-coded for quick scanning

Lead Assignment: Manager manually assigns leads to sales executives

Activity Timeline: Every call, WhatsApp message, note logged against the lead

Follow-Up Reminders: Scheduled reminders so no lead is forgotten. Missed follow-ups flagged to manager

Deal Lost: When a deal is lost, salesperson MUST select a reason (price too high, chose competitor, changed mind, poor experience, etc.)

Lead Source Tracking: Know exactly which marketing channel brings the most revenue
Goal: Create an enquiry in under 45 seconds. No lead falls through the cracks.

MODULE 3: Quotation Builder

Create quotation from lead -- select services/packages, system auto-fills pricing based on car segment

4 Package Tiers: Essential / Enhanced / Elite / Luxe -- per vertical, per car segment

Car Segments: Hatchback, Sedan, SUV, Luxury -- different pricing for each

Version Control: Every revision saved. You can see V1, V2, V3 and what changed

Discount Control:

Sales exec can give up to 5% without approval

Anything above 5% requires Owner approval (system blocks it until approved)

Every discount requires a reason code

Quotation PDF: Clean, professional PDF generated instantly for sharing with customer

Validity: Configurable expiry period (e.g., 7 days)

Leakage Tracking: Compare quoted value vs final invoiced value -- catch revenue leakage

MODULE 4: Booking & Advance Payment

Booking created from accepted quotation

70% Advance Rule: System enforces minimum 70% advance before job card can be created

Manager can override with documented reason (audit logged)

Stock Reservation: When a booking is confirmed, required materials are reserved in inventory

Promised Delivery Date: Captured at booking for tracking delays later

Payment Methods: Cash, UPI, Card, Bank Transfer, EMI -- all tracked separately

MODULE 5: Job Card & Workshop Management

Job card auto-created from booking (or manually for walk-in emergencies)

Vehicle Intake Form:

Registration number, odometer reading

Body condition map: existing scratches, dents, warning lights

Fuel level, belongings inventory, spare parts check

Customer concerns captured

Technician Assignment: Manager assigns one or multiple technicians per job

Task Breakdown: Sub-tasks by vertical (e.g., audio wiring, damping installation, seat stitching)

Time Tracking: Start/stop timer per task -- know exactly how long each job takes

Material Consumption: Technician logs materials used -- auto-deducted from inventory

Mandatory Photos: 4-6 photos required per job (before / during / after)

Progress Tracking: Percentage completion visible to manager at a glance

Supervisor Notes: Internal notes section for special instructions or observations

Mobile-Friendly Technician View: Big buttons, minimal typing, photo-first interface for workshop floor use

Job Status Flow: Created → In Progress → Pending QC → QC Passed → Ready for Billing → Ready for Delivery → Delivered

MODULE 6: Quality Control (QC)

QC Checklists: Customizable checklist per vertical (you define the items)

Inspection Form: Pass/Fail for each item, with notes and photos

Supervisor Sign-Off: Mandatory -- no delivery without QC approval

Rework Flow: If QC fails, job goes back to technician with specific notes on what needs fixing

Delivery Checklist:

Car cleaned

Demo given to customer

Invoice explained

Warranty card handed over

Old parts returned (if applicable)

Payment confirmed

Hard Lock: System will NOT allow car delivery without QC sign-off. No exceptions (unless Owner overrides with audit trail).

MODULE 7: Invoicing & Payments

Invoice auto-generated from completed job card

GST Calculation: CGST + SGST auto-calculated (single GSTIN)

Professional Invoice PDF with all line items, GST breakup, payment terms

Payment Recording:

Track payments by method (Cash / UPI / Card / EMI)

Partial payment tracking (70% advance + 30% final)

Payment receipt generation

Hard Locks:

No car release without payment rule satisfied

No invoice edit after payment without Owner override (audit logged)

Collection Aging: See overdue payments at a glance

Receivable Tracking: Who owes how much, for how long

MODULE 8: Quality Certificate

Auto-generated after full payment + QC pass

Contents:

Customer name and contact

Vehicle details

Complete work summary (what was done)

QC inspection confirmation

Warranty terms per service

Serial Numbering: Sequential (0001, 0002...)

PDF Download: For physical printing and digital sharing

Linked to job card and invoice for easy reference

MODULE 9: Inventory Management

Product Master: ~200 SKUs with brand, unit (pieces), and serial number tracking where needed

Stock View: Current quantities at a glance

Auto-Deduction: When technician logs material usage in job card, stock auto-reduces

Stock Reservation: Booked materials reserved, not available for other jobs

Transaction History: Full log of every stock movement (in/out/reserved/adjusted)

Supplier Returns: Track defective/warranty returns to vendors

Customer-Supplied Parts: Flag when customer brings their own parts, charge installation only

Tally Export: Inventory reports exportable as CSV/Excel for manual Tally import

MODULE 10: Fault / Comeback Tracking

When a customer returns with an issue after delivery, it's logged against the original job

Captured Information:

Complaint date, issue category, severity

Root cause analysis

Action taken, cost to company

Resolution date

Comeback Rate Metric: Per 100 deliveries -- tracked by category and by technician

Issue Flow: Reported → Under Review → Rework Scheduled → Resolved → Closed
Purpose: Identify repeat problems, hold technicians accountable, improve quality over time.

MODULE 11: WhatsApp Communication Log

Log all WhatsApp conversations per customer inside the CRM

Template Messages (ready to copy-paste):

Booking confirmation

Job started notification

Job completed notification

Invoice sharing

Follow-up messages

Birthday wishes

Communication timeline visible on every lead/customer page

Future-Ready: When you set up WhatsApp Business API later, these templates become auto-send triggers with one click

MODULE 12: Tally Export

Manual Export approach (as requested):

Export sales invoices as CSV/Excel in Tally-compatible format

Export payment receipts

Export purchase/inventory data

Export GST reports (GSTR-1 compatible)

One-click export for any date range

Future-Ready: System architecture supports adding automatic Tally connector later if you want real-time sync

MODULE 13: Dashboards & Reports

Owner Dashboard (single screen, real-time):

Today's enquiries, bookings, collections

Active job cards with progress

Pending deliveries and pending QC

Open faults and overdue follow-ups

High-discount deals flagged

Reports Available:

Daily / Weekly / Monthly sales

Salesperson performance: leads handled, conversions, revenue generated, discount % given

Technician productivity: jobs completed, average time per job, comeback rate

Lead source ROI: which marketing channel brings the most revenue per rupee spent

Conversion funnel: how many leads convert at each stage, where they drop off

Revenue by vertical (Audio vs Interior vs PPF vs OEM vs Custom vs Lighting)

Promised vs actual delivery dates (delay tracking)

Inventory status and dead stock

Quality score: total jobs vs faults

Collection aging and receivables
All reports filterable by date range, vertical, salesperson, technician.

MODULE 14: Basic HR (Attendance & Salary)

Staff list with roles and salary information

Basic attendance tracking: Present / Absent / Half-Day

Monthly salary records

Staff payment tracking

System Rules (Hard Locks -- Cannot Be Bypassed)

These rules are enforced by the system. No one can skip them without Owner override (which is logged):

No job card without approved quotation and 70% advance received

No delivery without QC sign-off

No car release without payment rule satisfied

No invoice edit after payment recording

No discount above 5% without Owner approval

Every discount requires a documented reason

Every financial edit is audit-logged (who, what, when)

Photos are mandatory for every job (minimum 4)

Deal lost requires a mandatory reason selection
