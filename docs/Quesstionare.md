## Section 4: Lead Management & Sales

- **Lead sources:**  
    - 90%: Instagram, Facebook, YouTube  
    - 10%: Google Ads, referrals, walk-ins
- **Current lead capture:** Unstructured (WhatsApp, phone calls, social media messages)  
- **Lead data collected:** Name, phone number, car model, service interest, approximate budget  
- **Lead assignment:** Manual (by manager)  
- **Sales cycle length:** Same day to several days/weeks  
- **Typical follow-ups:** 1–5  
- **Follow-up channels:** WhatsApp, phone calls  
- **Automation need:** Yes (reminders + automated follow-ups)  
- **Lead stages:**
    - **Hot:** Ready to book/pay advance
    - **Warm:** Interested, still deciding
    - **Cold:** Unsure/thinking
- **Common lost reasons:** Price concerns, product/quality concerns, overall experience, changed mind, cheaper competitor
